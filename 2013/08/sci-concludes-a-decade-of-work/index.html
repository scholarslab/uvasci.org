SCI concludes a decade of work

Posted on August 20th, 2013 | Categorized as News

As 10 years of SCI come to an end, we are grateful to the Mellon Foundation and our many partners at the University of Virginia and elsewhere for their support and collaboration. We invite you to look back on reports from our annual summer institutes (2004-2011), as well as from our 2012-2013 work on Scholarly Production, Graduate Education, and the Value of the Humanities in the Digital Age. Many thanks to all who have supported and worked with us over the years.
